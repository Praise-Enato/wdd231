Website Planning Document

Site Name

Neriss Hair Penache

Domain Suggestion: neriss-hair-penache.com or neriss-wigs.com

Reason for Selection: The name "Neriss Hair Penache" combines a unique personal brand identifier with the French word "penache," meaning flair or style, which perfectly represents the expertise and stylish approach to wig care and maintenance. This name establishes both professionalism and personality, making it memorable for clients seeking quality wig services.

Site Purpose

Neriss Hair Penache serves as a comprehensive digital platform dedicated to wig education, maintenance, and sales. The website provides expert guidance on wig care techniques, educational resources about different wig types and materials, and professional styling advice. Additionally, it functions as an e-commerce platform offering carefully curated wigs with detailed specifications and care instructions. The site bridges the gap between professional hair styling expertise and accessible wig care education, empowering users to maintain their wigs properly while providing a trusted source for quality wig purchases.

Typography

Primary Font: Arial (Sans-serif)

Used for: Headings, Navigation, Call-to-Action Elements

Arial provides clean, modern readability perfect for web interfaces and important interactive elements. Its sans-serif nature ensures excellent legibility across all devices.

Secondary Font: Georgia (Serif)

Used for: Body Text, Paragraphs, Content Areas

Georgia offers elegant readability for longer text content. Its serif design adds sophistication while maintaining excellent screen readability, perfect for educational content and detailed descriptions.

Supporting Font: Verdana (Sans-serif)

Used for: Captions, Form Labels, Fine Print

Verdana serves as a backup and utility font for smaller text elements, ensuring consistent readability in form elements and supplementary content.

Content Strategy

Page Structure (3 Pages Total)

  • Home (index.html): Welcome, services overview, featured products, testimonials
  • Care Guide: Comprehensive wig maintenance tutorials, styling tips, product recommendations
  • Shop: Wig catalog with detailed specifications, care instructions, and purchasing options
  • Contact Form Action Page: Form submission confirmation and contact details

JavaScript Functionality

  • Data Integration: Product catalog from JSON/API displaying 15+ wigs with 4+ properties each
  • Local Storage: User preferences, shopping cart, favorite care tips
  • Modal Dialogs: Product details, care instruction overlays, consultation booking
  • Interactive Elements: Care tip search/filter, product categories, responsive navigation
