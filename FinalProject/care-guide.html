Wig Care & Maintenance Tips

Empower yourself with the knowledge to keep your wigs fresh, stylish, and long-lasting.

Beginner Wig Care Steps

  • Washing: Use sulfate-free shampoo, lukewarm water, and gently finger comb while washing.
  • Drying: Pat dry with a towel, then air-dry on a wig stand. Avoid wringing or harsh heat.
  • Detangling: Use wide-tooth combs starting from the tips up to the roots to avoid pulling strands.
  • Storage: Always store on a stand to maintain shape and prevent tangling.

Popular Care Tips

Heat Styling Do's

Use heat tools only on heat-resistant wigs, and apply protectants before curling or straightening.

Night Care

Never sleep with your wig on. Always brush gently and store properly overnight.

Wig Cap Hygiene

Wash your wig cap regularly to avoid scalp irritation and wig odor.

Product Build-up

Avoid excessive oils and sprays. Clean build-up regularly to maintain natural movement and sheen.
